KYC / AML Onboarding Agent

A trustworthy onboarding decision for UAE banking: it retrieves the policy, cites every claim, refuses when the policy is silent, and grades its own answer with a second, independent model.

The problem

Onboarding a thin-file newcomer is high-stakes, and mostly manual.

A new arrival in the UAE has no local credit history and a thin document file. Onboarding them is high stakes and a wrong call is a compliance risk. Officers need a fast, consistent, defensible decision, not a slow manual review.

The product

Grounded, cited, and built to escalate.

The agent returns proceed, request documents, or escalate. It retrieves only the relevant policy sections, decides using only those, cites every claim to its section, refuses and escalates when the policy is silent, and routes any sanctions or PEP hit to a human. It never auto-approves.

The product calls I made
01
Never auto-approve a sanctions or PEP hit. Any match routes to a human, always.
02
Refuse and escalate over guessing. When the policy is silent, the agent says so instead of inventing an answer.
03
A second, independent model grades every answer. It scores each response against a rubric and tags why it failed: bad retrieval versus bad generation.
04
Proof the trust layer works. A built-in discrimination test feeds the judge deliberately broken answers and shows it catches them.
